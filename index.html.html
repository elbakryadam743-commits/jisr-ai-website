Medicine works best when the doctor and patient arrive at the same moment fully prepared. Jisr exists to make that possible: removing friction, capturing what matters, and ensuring every consultation begins with clarity on both sides.

What is Jisr AI?
"Jisr" — Arabic for bridge (جسر)

We chose this name deliberately. A bridge closes the distance between two points. Jisr AI closes the distance between an unprepared consultation and an informed one, making every visit more efficient and more meaningful for both the physician and the patient.

Jisr AI is the first point of interaction between your patient and their care, before they walk through the door.
The Problem

Physicians are under impossible pressure.
The history is always rushed and incomplete.

Incomplete history. Incomplete diagnosis.
The history problem
The history is always rushed and incomplete.

No time to listen

With ~10 minutes per patient and up to 50% of that consumed by history-taking and typing, a physician has barely 5 minutes of actual clinical focus per consultation.

History is the foundation of diagnosis — and it is being rushed

Up to 80% of diagnoses are made from patient history alone. When history-taking is compressed under time pressure, findings get missed. Missed findings lead to missed diagnoses. A rushed history is not a minor inconvenience. It is a clinical risk.

Every minute typing is a minute stolen from the patient.
The documentation problem

Every consultation, typed from scratch

Physicians manually re-enter the same patient data, visit after visit. Demographic details, medical history, chief complaints: all typed by hand, every time. Hours of a physician's week consumed by repetitive data entry that delivers zero clinical value.

Documentation overload leads to burnout

The cognitive cost of constant manual typing doesn't just waste time — it drains the mental energy physicians need most. Documentation burden is one of the leading drivers of physician burnout. Every minute at the keyboard is attention taken away from the patient sitting across the desk.

Time saved on documentation is time given back to the patient — and to better care.

The Solution

Two problems. One platform.

Pre-Visit History

Jisr conducts a structured, bilingual patient interview before every consultation. Chief complaints, medical history, medications, allergies: all collected, organised, and flagged automatically.

The physician receives a complete clinical report with provisional considerations before the patient walks in. No more starting from scratch.

The patient's full story, waiting for you before the consultation begins.

Automatic Documentation

The patient file is built before you touch the keyboard. History structured, findings flagged, file formatted and saved directly to your EHR, all automatically, before the consultation begins.

No more typing patient files manually. No more splitting attention between the patient and the screen.

Doctors only type what matters — their clinical judgment.
